on this page there are a bunch of jokes.

Warning these are all very cheesey. You might not to read them all at once, or risk over exposure to cringe.

  1. J. What do you call a dinosaur made of cheese?

    A. Gorgonzilla

  2. J. What do you call a cheese that isn’t yours?

    A. Nacho cheese.

  3. J.Which cheese surrounds a medieval castle?

    A. Moat-zarella.

  4. J. My wife was preparing lunch today when she asked, “Honey, where’s the cheese grater?”

    A. I replied, “Some would say France, others would say England. It depends on your personal preference.”

  5. J. Why does cheese look sensible?

    A. Because everyone else on the plate is crackers.

  6. J. The other day this guy threatened to throw dough, cheese and tomatoes at me.

    A. I said, “You wanna pizza me?”

  7. J. Which cheese is made backwards?

    A. Edam.

  8. J. Why didn’t the cheese want to get sliced?

    A. It had grater plans.

  9. J. What do you call a cheese with curly hair?

    A. Perm-esan.

  10. J. When should you go on a cheese diet?

    A. When you need to Cheddar few pounds.
